• Ingredients
    1. whole wheat flour
    2. oil
    3. water
    4. chopped vegetables
    5. salt
    6. basic spices
  • Method:
    1. knead a soft dough with water and tablesppon of oil.
    2. Chop your desired veggies(onion,capsicum,cabbage).
    3. Saute them in a hot pan with some spices(black pepper, red pepper, salt and teaspoon vinegar)
    4. now put the veggeis or filling to rest for some time.
      Meanwhile prepare small round balls of dough for momo covering.
    5. Now fill the sheet with the veggies and then steam for 10 minutes.
    6. Serve hot with red chutney.

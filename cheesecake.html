• Ingredients
    1. Digestive biscuit
    2. cream cheese
    3. sugar
    4. Whipped cream
  • Method:
    1. In a bowl add cream cheese, whipped cream and sugar.
    2. Mix them well using a blender.
    3. Now add milk and make a smooth consistent batter.
    4. make sure batter is not very liquidy.
      After that pour batter into a tray and set it to steam for 10-12 minutes.
    5. Add topings of your choice and serve chilled.

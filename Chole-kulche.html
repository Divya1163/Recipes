• Ingredients
    1. chole
    2. whole wheat flour
    3. baking powder
    4. baking soda
    5. basic spices and salt
  • Method:
    1. Knead the dough for the kulcha with water and tablespoon of oil.
    2. Boil the chole with teaspoon baking powder and baking soda.
      make sure the chole are soft and can be easily separated from its cover.
    3. Now comes the tadka part.
    4. prepare a sauce for the chole with spices and oil in hot vessel.
    5. prepare kulcha and serve hot with pyaaz and aachar.
